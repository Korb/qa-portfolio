# Translation of Documentation on Setting up a CNC Lathe  

> **Note:** This is a fragment of a test assignment completed for an engineering company involved in, among other things, the import and registration of industrial equipment into Russia.  

**Document Type:** Machine setup guide  
**Audience:** Line workers  
**Original Language:** English, Chinese  
**Target Language:** Russian  

---  

The source material was a machine translation from Chinese into English; the task was to produce a Russian-language version suitable for publication. The accompanying notes (in Russian) cover terminology decisions, style choices, unresolved ambiguities, and recommendations for further work.
